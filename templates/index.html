HOW

Our database stores all your information. All your events and meetings will be recorded, and they can be accessed at any time! As a user, you can check attendance from previous meetings, read feedback from surveys, and view other information that is beneficial to your organization. Organization Planner also quickly creates invitations, notifications, or surveys for you by using Google forms. With our website, any organization can setup events.

WHY

Creating events and meetings and recording attendance can be tiresome. Organization Planner eases this burden. This web application creates invitations and surveys for users, making the first step of organizing an even quick and easy. This application also records data from meetings, which users can access at any time. With Organization Planner, organizations can quickly get the word out and have more time to plan their memorable events.
